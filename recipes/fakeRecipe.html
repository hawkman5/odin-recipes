This recipe makes Real fake food

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp. salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 Tbsp. butter, melted
  • 3-6 ripe medium bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (325 degrees F if you are making a loaf of bread).
  2. Combine flour, soda, and salt in a medium bowl and set that aside.
  3. Mix egg, sugar, and butter in a large mixing bowl. Once mixed, add your mashed bananas and mix further. Combine this with your flour mixture. At this point, add your nuts if you so choose(I don't like mine with nuts so I skip this step).
  4. Pour your mixture into a greased muffin pan (or a greased six cup bread loaf pan).
  5. Bake muffins for 25-30 minutes. If making bread, bake for 60-75 minutes.
  6. Let the muffins cool for at least 10 minutes before enjoying them!
